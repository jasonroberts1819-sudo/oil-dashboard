Methodology & sources

Balance identity (EIA Petroleum Supply Monthly): field production + transfers to crude supply + imports + adjustment = refinery & blender net inputs + exports + stock change (incl. SPR). Basis: Jan–Apr 2026 are PSM monthly actuals; May–Jun are WPSR weekly-implied; the July bar is a single weekly report (partial — shown hatched, not a full month). Cushing is weekly-derived throughout (EIA has no monthly Cushing series). The 2018–2025 average line is computed from EIA series MCRSCUS1 (monthly total-crude stock change), pulled 2026-07-13 and verified against the 2026 PSM actuals (May+Jun avg = −22.2 mmbbl).
How this updates as new data lands
Data lives in the DATA object at the top of this file's script. To refresh: re-run the EIA pull in reference data/ (series MCRSCUS1 for total-crude change; WPSR weekly levels for May–Jul implieds), then update the usTotal2026, cushing2026 and levels values. The May PSM print (lands 31-Jul) is the first hard check on the weekly-implied May figure. Numbers here reflect the vintage available on the "as of" date; weekly-implied months typically land 1–3 mmbbl from the monthly survey.
